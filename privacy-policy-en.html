2. WHAT DATA I COLLECT AND WHY

Processing is different for free and Premium users:

A) FOR ALL USERS (free and Premium):

  • Automatic technical data: Device type, operating system, App version, IP address (anonymized), usage times, features used. This data does NOT personally identify you.
  • Advertising data (free version only): The App displays ads via Google AdMob and Meta Audience Network. To show relevant ads, your device's advertising ID (AAID/IDFA) and general usage data may be collected. Ad personalization can be disabled in your device settings.
  • Device permissions:
    • Storage/Photos (optional): Required to save estimate PDFs to your device and share them (WhatsApp, email, etc.) and to add a logo from gallery or camera. Photos used as logos are NEVER uploaded to our servers or saved to the cloud; they are only used locally to generate the PDF.

B) PREMIUM USERS ONLY:

  • Account: To save estimates to the cloud and sync across devices, you need to create an account with an email address. We only save your email for login purposes.
  • Estimate data (voluntarily provided by YOU): When you create an estimate and choose to save it to the cloud, the data you enter is stored on secure Google Firebase (Firestore) servers. This may include:
    • Your information: Name, surname, phone number, VAT number, Tax ID (Italy), CPF/CNPJ (Brazil), RUT (Chile), CUIT/CUIL (Argentina), business email, logo.
    • Client information: Name, surname, phone number, VAT/Tax ID/CPF/CNPJ/RUT/CUIT, address.
    • Estimate details: Services, prices, VAT, notes.
Important: As a FREE user, no personal data leaves your device. Everything is saved locally (local storage) and only you have access to it.

5. ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE (AI) - GOOGLE GEMINI

The App offers an optional AI analysis feature for estimates and price lists via Google Gemini API (Google's artificial intelligence service).

What is sent to the AI:

  • Prices and descriptions of the jobs entered in the estimate/price list
  • Detected business sector (e.g., construction, plumbing, electrical)
  • Country and currency selected by the user
  • Quantities and numbers of jobs (for analysis purposes only)

What is NEVER sent to the AI:

  • Personal data (name, surname, email, phone number, VAT number, address)
  • Client data (name, phone number, address)
  • Geographic coordinates or precise location
  • Payment data or sensitive financial information
  • Device ID or unique identifiers

How it works:

  • AI analysis is only for Premium users (after subscription purchase)
  • The user must actively click the "Analyze with AI" button
  • No automatic analysis is performed without your explicit consent
  • Analyses are processed in real-time and are not stored by Google Gemini after processing
  • Only anonymous logs (date, number of requests, success/error) are saved for service improvement

Where data is processed:

AI requests are handled via Google Cloud Functions and Google Gemini API, which may process data on servers located in:

  • United States (us-central1 region, Iowa)
  • Europe (europe-west3 region, Frankfurt) - preferred for EU/EEA/UK/Switzerland users

Data transfers to the US are conducted in compliance with the Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) of the European Commission and the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

Limitations:

  • AI analyses are limited to 5 during the first 7 days of Premium, then 50 per month
  • The limit resets automatically every month
  • You can see how many analyses you have remaining directly in the App

Disabling:

The AI feature is completely optional. If you do not wish to use it, simply do not click the button. The App works perfectly without AI analysis.

More information:

For details on how Google handles data when using Gemini, see:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Cloud Data Processing Terms

7. DATA RETENTION (HOW LONG WE KEEP IT)

  • Free users: Locally saved data remains on your device until you delete the App or data. We do not store it.
  • Premium users: Your data (email and estimates) is stored as long as you keep your account active. Upon account deletion, data is removed from our servers within:
    • 30 days for GDPR and European regulations
    • 15 business days for LGPD (Brazil) upon request
    • Reasonable period for POPIA (South Africa) and South American laws
  • Anonymous technical data: Stored for a maximum of 24 months for aggregate analysis.
  • Data for legal obligations: Stored for the period required by tax and commercial laws of each country (typically 5-10 years).

9. DATA SECURITY

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Firebase Security Rules: Data access only for authorized users
  • Encryption: Data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (Firestore encryption)
  • Limited access: Only me (Alessio Di Pede) has administrative access
  • Regular risk assessment and appropriate measures
  • Encrypted backups on secure servers

However, no system is 100% immune. In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights:

  • GDPR: Notification to the Authority within 72 hours and to you without undue delay
  • LGPD: Communication to ANPD and data subjects within "reasonable time"
  • POPIA: Notification to the Information Regulator and data subjects immediately
  • Argentina: Notification to the Dirección Nacional de Protección de Datos Personales

10. COOKIES AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES

The App does not use traditional cookies. We use:
