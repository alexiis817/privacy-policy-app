2. WHAT DATA I COLLECT AND WHY

The processing is different for free and Premium users:

A) FOR ALL USERS (free and Premium):

  • Automatic technical data: Device type, operating system, App version, IP address (anonymized), usage times, functions used. This data does NOT personally identify you.
  • Advertising data (free version only): The App displays ads via Google AdMob and Meta Audience Network. To show relevant ads, the device's advertising ID (AAID/IDFA) and general usage data may be collected. Ad personalization can be disabled in your device settings.
  • Device permissions:
    • Storage/Photos (optional): Required to save estimate PDFs to your device and share them (WhatsApp, email, etc.) and to add a logo from gallery or camera. Photos used as logos are NEVER uploaded to our servers or saved to the cloud; they are used only locally to generate the PDF.

B) PREMIUM USERS ONLY:

  • Account: To save estimates in the cloud and sync between devices, you need to create an account with an email address. We only save the email for access.
  • Estimate data (voluntarily provided by YOU): When you create an estimate and choose to save it to the cloud, the data you enter is stored on Google Firebase's secure servers (Firestore). This may include:
    • Your information: Name, surname, phone number, NIF/CIF (Spain), P.IVA/CF (Italy), CPF/CNPJ (Brazil), RUT (Chile), CUIT/CUIL (Argentina), NIT (Colombia), RUC (Peru), business email, logo.
    • Customer information: Name, surname, phone, NIF/CIF/CPF/CNPJ/RUT/CUIT/NIT/RUC, address.
    • Estimate details: Services, prices, VAT/IVA/IGV, notes.
Important: As a FREE user, no personal data leaves your device. Everything is saved locally (local storage) and only you have access.

4. WHO WE SHARE DATA WITH

We NEVER sell your data. We share it only with essential providers ("Processors"):

  • Google Firebase (Google Cloud Platform): Hosts the database (Firestore) where Premium users save estimates. Servers are secure and located in EU/US depending on region. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob & Meta Audience Network: To manage advertising. Meta Privacy Policy.
  • Payment stores: If you purchase Premium through Apple App Store or Google Play Store, they handle payment data. I never receive your credit card.
  • RevenueCat (optional): I may use this service to manage Premium subscriptions. RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

We have contractual agreements with all these providers requiring them to protect your data and use it only according to our instructions.

6. DATA RETENTION (HOW LONG WE KEEP DATA)

  • Free users: Data saved locally remains on your device until you delete the App or the data. We do not retain it.
  • Premium users: Your data (email and estimates) is retained while you maintain an active account. Upon account cancellation, data is deleted from our servers within:
    • 30 days for GDPR and European standards
    • 15 business days for LGPD (Brazil) upon request
    • Reasonable period for POPIA (South Africa) and South American laws
    • 10 days for Law 1581 (Colombia)
  • Anonymous technical data: Retained for maximum 24 months for aggregate analysis.
  • Data for legal obligations: Retained for the period required by tax and commercial laws of each country (typically 5-10 years).

8. DATA SECURITY

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Firebase Security Rules: Data access only for authorized users
  • Encryption: Data in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest (Firestore encryption)
  • Limited access: Only I (Alessio Di Pede) have administrative access
  • Periodic risk assessment and appropriate measures
  • Encrypted backups on secure servers

However, no system is 100% immune. In case of a data breach involving risk to your rights:

  • GDPR: Notification to Authority within 72 hours and to you without undue delay
  • LGPD: Communication to ANPD and data subjects in "reasonable time"
  • POPIA: Notification to Information Regulator and data subjects immediately
  • Argentina: Notification to National Directorate of Personal Data Protection
  • Colombia: Notification to Superintendency of Industry and Commerce
  • Spain: Notification to AEPD within 72 hours

9. COOKIES AND SIMILAR TECHNOLOGIES

The App does not use traditional cookies. We use:

  • Local Storage: To save preferences and estimates locally (on your device)
  • Device identifiers: For anonymous analytics (Crashlytics) and advertising (AdMob)
  • Tracking pixels: Only to measure ad performance (Meta, Google)

You can manage advertising personalization preferences in your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID / Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Huawei devices: Settings > Privacy > Advertising & Privacy
